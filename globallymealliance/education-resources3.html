What parents need to know…

Teach Your Kids to Be Tick AWARE!

Summer camp and other outdoor activities offer fun, new experiences, and great memories for kids and teens.
Don’t let a tick make your kid sick!

Make tick checks a part of their (and yours too) daily routine! Its an important habit!

Important Tips - When sending your kids to camp or other activity.

  • Find out if the camp treats the grounds for ticks or other insects.
  • Protect your kids with pre-treated (permethrin) clothing or with repellent. Discuss your preferred safety measures with camp staff.
  • Ask if camp counselors or other staff receive tick prevention training.
  • Do they teach kids tick prevention and awareness.

Free GLA Tick AWARE Prevention Kits

Be Tick AWARE! Reduce the risk of Lyme and other tick-borne diseases. Take important steps to educate your staff about the importance of tick bite prevention.
